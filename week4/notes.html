Chapter 12

This chapter focused on object-oriented programming. From what I understood from this chapter and a few videos is that it is basically a style of programming in a way. It also mentions three main concepts: Encapsulation- This one is basically things being kept simple on the outside while the inner things are kept hidden. An example is used for an ‘ON BUTTON’. Polymorphism- This basically means that the same process can be used for different objects hence the poly in the word. Inheritance- Basically it means to add more things into something or more features.
